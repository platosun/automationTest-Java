The Commbank app for tablet is our free mobile banking and payments app, designed for iPads. It offers a fast, convenient and secure tablet banking experience. The CommBank app for tablet is available to CommBank customers registered for NetBank with NetCode SMS.

Using the CommBank app for tablet you can:

  • Check the balances of your accounts
  • View up to two years of account transaction history
  • Set up and edit your savings tracker
  • Activate a new card
  • Set or change a card PIN
  • Transfer money between your accounts
  • Transfer money to other accounts
  • View, set up and delete scheduled payments
  • Pay your bills using BPAY
  • View your assets and liabilities in portfolio view

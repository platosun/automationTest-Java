To use the CommBank app for tablet you’ll need:

  • Your NetBank client number, password and be registered for NetCode SMS;
  • An iPad (with iOS version 7.0 or more recent) enabled for cellular or wireless internet connection
  • Your mobile phone and an active mobile phone number to receive the Netcode SMS

If you’re not registered for NetBank or NetCode SMS, please call us on 13 2221 at any time to set this up.

Registration

The first time you log on to the CommBank app you'll need to complete the following steps:

  1. Enter your NetBank client number and password.
  2. Generate a NetCode SMS to verify your mobile phone number.
  3. Choose your main account.
  4. Set a 4-digit PIN for fast log on (optional).
  5. Accept the Terms and Conditions.
